# Product Overview

eSIM Myanmar Entertainment Server is an enterprise eSIM management platform serving the Myanmar and ASEAN markets.

## Core Purpose
- eSIM profile management with QR code activation
- 5G/VoLTE network support with international roaming
- Cross-device eSIM transfer (Android to Apple, iOS Quick Transfer)
- Entertainment services (TV, movies, gaming, music streaming)
- Multi-device support (iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, Android, Wearables)

## User Roles
- **Customer**: eSIM activation, usage tracking, payments, device management, profile transfer
- **Partner**: Affiliate/reseller dashboard, commission tracking, API access
- **Admin**: User management, inventory, analytics, system configuration, support management

## Target Market
- Myanmar primary market (MMK currency, local payment integrations)
- ASEAN regional expansion
- Enterprise and consumer segments
- Tourist and traveler plans

## Key Integrations
- Payment: KBZ Pay, Wave Money, AYA Pay
- Network: SM-DP+ GSMA compliant
- Auth: JWT, Refresh tokens, 2FA/TOTP support

## eSIM Core Flows
- Phone number registration with KYC
- eSIM purchase and provisioning
- QR code generation and delivery
- Profile activation via QR scan
- SIM to eSIM migration
- Android to Apple transfer
- iOS Quick Transfer
- Device upgrade and swap
- Wearable support (Apple Watch)
- International roaming management
